About Us

World Institute For Research In Advanced Sciences (WIRAS), being found in 2017 with the noble aim of molding an exalted group of Islamic scholars who learn and research in classical Islamic texts along with modern academic disciplines.

Located at Markaz Knowledge City, The biggest integrated township in south India,WIRAS offers unparalleled opportunities in multidisciplinary academic approaches with classical Islamic knowledge tradition. WIRAS fosters the uniqueness in right for education, intellectual independence, originality of resources, tradition with innovative and advanced researches for the sake of upgrading humanity with moral values, adhering to religious perspectives.
